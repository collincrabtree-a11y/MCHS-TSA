About Us

McCreary County Government serves the community with transparency, accountability, and excellence. Our elected officials work collaboratively to meet the needs of residents and provide effective governance.

  • Fiscal Court meets monthly to set policies, approve budgets, and oversee county claims.
  • Officials collaborate to maintain transparency and community engagement.
  • Responsible budgeting ensures essential public services are fully supported.
  • Multiple elected offices—including Clerk, Sheriff, Attorney, Coroner, Jailer, and PVA—serve citizens efficiently.
  • Public records are accessible under the Open Records Act for full transparency.
